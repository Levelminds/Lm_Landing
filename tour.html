How It Works: A Simple Guide

1. Create Your Profile

For Schools: Register your institution in minutes. Provide details about your school's culture, values, and the types of roles you typically hire for.
For Teachers: Build your free digital portfolio. Go beyond the resume by adding your teaching philosophy, classroom videos, and project examples.

2. Post & Discover Opportunities

For Schools: Post unlimited job openings for free. Use our smart tagging to specify required skills, subjects, and grade levels to attract the right candidates.
For Teachers: Browse job listings tailored to your skills and preferences. Apply with a single click using your completed LevelMinds portfolio.

3. Manage & Track Everything

For Schools: Move candidates through your hiring pipeline. Review, shortlist, and schedule interviews with your team, all in one place.
For Teachers: Say goodbye to the application "black hole." Your dashboard shows the exact status of every application, so you always know where you stand.
